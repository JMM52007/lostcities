Sophie's Family

                                      Sophie Elizabeth Foster

                                      Sophie is a Telepath, Inflictor, Polygot, Teleporter, and Enhancer. She has five bodyguards. Sandor(goblin), Flori(gnome), Bo(ogre), Tarina(troll), Nubiti(dwarf). She sleeps with a stuffed animal elephant named Ella.

                                      Amy Rose Foster(Natalie Freeman)

                                      Amy is Sophie's human sister. She has seen the Lost Cities and has lived there for a while. She has a stuffed animal bunny named BunBun.

                                      Edaline Kelia Ruewen

                                      Edaline is a Conjurer. She is Sophie's adopted mother. She has a goblin bodyguard named Cadoc.

                                      Emma Iris Foster(Kate Freeman)

                                      Emma is Sophie's human mom. She has been to the Lost Cities but doesn't remember.

                                      Grady Howell Ruewen

                                      Grady is a Mesmer. He is Sophie's adopted father. He used to have a goblin bodyguard named Brielle.

                                      Jolie Lucine Ruewen

                                      Jolie died in a fire set by Brant, a member of the Neverseen.

                                      William David Foster(Conner Freeman)

                                      William is Sophie's human dad. He has been to the Lost Cities but doesn't remember.
